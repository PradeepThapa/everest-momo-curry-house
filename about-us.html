Our Story

Namaste ! Welcome to Everest Momo and Curry House, where every dish is a journey to the heart of the Himalayas. We are Kishan and Kamala, a husband-and-wife team with a passion for bringing the authentic flavors of Nepal to our new home.

At Everest Momo and Curry House, Kishan meticulously crafts each dish. Our signature Mo:Mo and aromatic curries are prepared using authentic spices and techniques, offering a taste of the vibrant streets of Kathmandu. Each bite is a celebration of tradition and taste, crafted with love and expertise to ensure a culinary experience like no other.

Join us and discover the pinnacle of Nepalese cuisine with our exquisite Mo:Mo, flavorful curries, and more.
