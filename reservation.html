Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What's on the Menu?

At our Everest Momo and Curry House, we specialize in mouthwatering Nepalese momos and fiery Indian curries that will transport your taste buds to the Himalayas and beyond! From traditional momo fillings to rich and aromatic curries, we've got a culinary adventure waiting for you.

Do You Offer Delivery?

Yes, we offer speedy delivery services straight to your doorstep, ensuring that you can enjoy our delectable dishes in the comfort of your own home. Say goodbye to hunger pangs and hello to a feast fit for a mountain explorer!

Any Vegan Options?

Absolutely! Our menu features a variety of vegan options that are bursting with flavor and authenticity. Indulge in plant-based delights that will make even the most dedicated carnivores reconsider their choices.

Can I Book a Table?

Reservations are highly recommended to secure your spot at our cozy and vibrant restaurant. Whether you're planning a romantic dinner for two or a lively gathering with friends, we'll ensure that your dining experience is nothing short of unforgettable.

What Sets You Apart?

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity, and culinary excellence. With a fusion of Nepalese and Indian flavors, each bite tells a story of tradition and passion, making every meal a journey to remember.
